HR - INTERNAL POLICY

Leave policy

PollenIT Technologies · Effective 01 April 2026 · Udaipur

12 days

Earned / paid leave

Available each calendar year. Unused earned leave may carry forward, up to 30 days.

3 days

Notice period

Submit requests at least three working days in advance, except in emergencies.

30 days

Carry-forward cap

Earned leave beyond this balance cannot be carried forward.

LWP

When paid leave ends

Leave without pay applies when paid leave entitlements are exhausted.

Approval and absence

Reporting-manager approval is required. Emergency leave must be communicated as early as possible. Absence without prior intimation is treated as LWP; repeated instances may lead to disciplinary action. HR Head and Management approval is required for policy deviations.

Public holidays

National holidays: Republic Day (26 Jan), Independence Day (15 Aug), Gandhi Jayanti (2 Oct). The company also observes five festival holidays selected from the published festival list.
